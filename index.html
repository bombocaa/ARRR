Augmented Reality Quick Demos

Two lightweight examples you can run locally:

model-viewer (Quick Look, Scene Viewer, WebXR)
Tap the AR button to launch native AR on iOS (Quick Look) or Android (Scene Viewer). Works on desktop with WebXR if available.
 AR.js Marker Tracking (Hiro)
Point your camera at the Hiro marker to see the model anchored to it. Uses your device camera in-browser.

Tip: Serve these files over HTTP. For example: npx serve -l 8080 . then open http://localhost:8080.
